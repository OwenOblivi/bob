Where DarkRP meets Team-Deathmatch

Info
This server was created back in 2012 to try and get away from stereotypical DarkRP tropes. Players are encouraged to fight enemy factions, get loot, and mod any of the 120+ guns available to their heart's content.

If this is your first time on this server, a Server Greeter will be meeting you on arrival in order to greet you and introduce you to the concept of this server.

Here are the basic rules.
A more detailed version can be found on our MOTD.

1. No prop blocking, pushing, climbing, etc. (Surfing is tolerated as long as you're a peaceful class)
2. No RDM (Random DeathMatch) except for Criminals VS Cops VS Zombies.
3. There is no NLR to follow on this server.
4. Fading doors obligatory. (Your base must be raidable)
5. No spawn camping, blocking, etc.
6. Criminals can raid and steal from vendors and other peaceful jobs, but not kill them.
7. Do not exploit unfair map areas. (Such as untextured floors on the top of the map)
8. Badass Fists are for fighting and breaking down doors during raids only, do not punch or break props for no reason.
9. No mic spamming (Or any general spam for that matter)
10. Don't disrespect other players, act like an ass, or do any kind of griefing in general.
11. Any type of hack/script/bot is not allowed.
12. Don't make scam trades in the gem market.
13. Don't build in the sky or in any other area that is unreachable without propclimbing or surfing.
14. Last but not least: Have fun!
